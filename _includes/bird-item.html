{{site.data.bird | size }} birds & counting...

I put this site together as a fun way of keeping track of the birds that my wife and I love to watch. I am limiting this list to birds we've seen land in my immediate yard and trees.

On the property, I have two bird feeders: one is located in a maple in the front yard, and one in a poplar in the back yard. Below the maple is a bird bath which the birds make frequent use of. I have a large, old cottonwood along with thick bushes that line the perimeter. All this, combined with a lack of pets, has made my small patch of earth quite attractive to local birds.

I am looking forward to growing this list as the years progress.
